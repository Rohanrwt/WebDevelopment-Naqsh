Find Us

Located in Mohanchatti, about 10 km from Rishikesh main town. Away from the noise, close to nature.

How to Reach

From Rishikesh

~25 mins drive via Badrinath Road. Turn towards Mohanchatti after Shivpuri.

From Dehradun

~1.5 hours via Haridwar-Rishikesh highway. Continue past Rishikesh towards Badrinath.

From Delhi

~6 hours drive (250 km). Take NH334 via Haridwar. Alternatively, train to Haridwar + taxi.
