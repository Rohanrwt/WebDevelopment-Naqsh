What's MAPAI?

MAPAI = Breakfast + Dinner included.

Simple home-style meals. No buffet chaos, no overpriced restaurant. Just honest food served at the resort.

Breakfast

Paranthas, eggs, toast, chai/coffee, seasonal fruits

Dinner

Dal, sabzi, roti, rice, salad. Non-veg available on request.

Lunch can be arranged separately. Just let us know in advance.
